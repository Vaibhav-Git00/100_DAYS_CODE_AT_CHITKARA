_______________Conditional statements_______________________

Loops/Iterative statements are used for repetition.
Types of Loops: While, For, Do-While
While Loop: while(condition){//code}
Loop variable: i, j, k, etc.
For Loop: for(init statement; condition; expression){//code}
Break Keyword: allows you to terminate and exit a loop (do while/for/while) or switch commands from any point other than the logical end.
Do-While Loop: do{//code}while(condition);
Continue KeywordL allows you to skip an iteration
